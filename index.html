About me

My current position as a Teacher of English has allowed close collaboration with my headmaster, colleagues, and students as well as many different departments. I have been able to coordinate several activities and different people in order to create a strong scholar community. The position of Teacher of English is a position where my interpersonal and organizational skills, and experience with so many types of students and places, has been productive and rewarding. Nevertheless, I'm currently seeking new opportunities related with the IT world. Speaking of position qualifications, concentrations in literature, speaking and writing in both my native language and English in my undergraduate program have allowed me to become a skilled writer and speaker. I have a C2 qualification given by the CAE exam of The University of Cambridge and I'm currently working towards getting a degree in Full Stack Development in Desafío Latam academy. Organization is essential when balancing multiple projects, and the ability to organize well has made my experience as a teacher and future developer more worthwhile. The greatest asset I offer is the ability to work with many personalities and groups of people, and I realize just how valuable is every person as part of a work team. I can think of no better way to honor the riches of my education and experience than to belong to your organization.
